About the App

Rezident Doma Kralovykh is a content automation tool designed for members of the Dom Kralovykh community. It enables seamless publishing of motivational and educational video content across major social media platforms.

The tool helps community members and partners share valuable insights about personal growth, financial success, and mindset development — reaching wider audiences automatically.

✂️

Smart Video Cutting

Automatically splits long videos into short clips optimized for Shorts and Reels format (9:16 vertical).

📤

Multi-Platform Publishing

Publishes content simultaneously to TikTok, YouTube, Telegram, Instagram and other platforms.

🎯

Community Growth

Helps Dom Kralovykh partners attract new members through consistent, high-quality video content.

🤖

AI Subtitles

Automatically generates and burns subtitles into videos using Whisper AI for maximum reach.
