Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Rezident Doma Kralovykh ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

— TikTok account access tokens (provided by TikTok OAuth, used solely to publish content on your behalf).

— Content files (video, text) that you choose to publish through the App.

We do not collect personal data such as name, email, or location from TikTok users who view published content.

3. How We Use Information

Access tokens are used exclusively to interact with the TikTok API to post content. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally in an environment configuration file on the operator's device. They are not transmitted to any external server controlled by us.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to operate the App. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App. To do so, contact us at the email below.
